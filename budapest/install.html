Install BudaPlan

Follow the steps below to add the BudaPlan app to your device.

For iOS Users

  1. On this page, tap the button at the bottom of the screen.
  2. Select Add to Home Screen.
  3. Confirm by tapping Add.

For Android Users

  1. On this page, tap the (three-dot menu) in the top-right corner.
  2. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  3. Follow the prompts to confirm.
