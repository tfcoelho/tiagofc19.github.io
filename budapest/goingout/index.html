Nightlife: Ruin Bars

DID YOU KNOW?

Budapest's ruin bars are famous for their eclectic charm and vibrant atmosphere. Two of the most iconic are Szimpla Kert and the Instant-Fogas Complex.

Szimpla Kert, the first ruin bar in Budapest, opened in 2002 and features quirky designs like mismatched furniture and a vintage Trabant car as seating.
