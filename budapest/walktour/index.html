DID YOU KNOW?

During a free walking tour in Budapest, a group once stumbled upon a hidden underground jazz club that wasn't on any map. The club, accessible through a secret door in an unassuming alley, was hosting an impromptu performance by a world-renowned musician who happened to be in town. The surprised tourists enjoyed an unforgettable, exclusive concert, all thanks to the serendipity of exploring the city's streets on foot.
