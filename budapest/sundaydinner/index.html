Sunday Dinner

DID YOU KNOW?

For a perfect Sunday evening, why not enjoy the best of both worlds? Start with a delightful Italian dinner at Teatro di Fragola, where authentic flavors meet an elegant ambiance. Then, indulge in the cozy charm of Gaby’s Budapest, known for its warm atmosphere and delicious desserts.

Teatro di Fragola is loved for its classic handmade pastas and pizzas, while Gaby’s takes pride in serving some of the best hot chocolate in Budapest!
