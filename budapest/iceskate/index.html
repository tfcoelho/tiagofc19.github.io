Városliget
Ice Rink

DID YOU KNOW?

The Városliget Ice Rink, established in 1870, is one of Europe's largest and oldest open-air ice rinks. Nestled between the picturesque Vajdahunyad Castle and Heroes’ Square, it offers skaters breathtaking views as they glide across the ice. During the summer months, the rink is transformed into a boating lake , providing year-round recreational activities in the heart of Budapest.
