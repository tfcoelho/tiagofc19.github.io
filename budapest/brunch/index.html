Twentysix Brunch

DID YOU KNOW?

The word "brunch" first appeared in an 1895 British publication, where it was described as a Sunday meal for Saturday night carousers. The idea was to combine the best of breakfast and lunch while allowing late risers to recover from their indulgent evenings.

Over time, brunch became a cultural phenomenon, symbolizing relaxation and indulgence, often featuring decadent dishes like pancakes, avocado toast, and mimosas. In Budapest, places like Twentysix Budapest bring brunch to life in unique ways, blending gastronomy with a lush, tropical atmosphere.
