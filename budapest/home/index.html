DID YOU KNOW?

Your home at Dob utca 63 is in Budapest's lively 7th district, known for its rich history and vibrant culture. Just a short walk away is the Dohány Street Synagogue, the largest in Europe, and a centerpiece of the historic Jewish Quarter. This area is famous for its unique mix of ruin bars, cobblestone streets, and layered history.

Fun fact: Dob Street was once a hub for craftspeople, and it’s said one shopkeeper used a louder bell to lure customers away from a rival bakery!
